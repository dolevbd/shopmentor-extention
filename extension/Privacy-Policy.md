**Privacy Policy for ShopMentor AI
Effective Date: January 22, 2026**

ShopMentor AI is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle data within our Chrome Extension.

**1. **Single Purpose****
  ShopMentor AI is designed to provide real-time AI-powered shopping advice and price comparisons directly on e-commerce product pages.

**2. Data Collection and Usage**
  Website Content: We analyze product details (titles, prices, and specifications) on supported e-commerce sites to generate AI insights. This data is processed in real-time and is not stored permanently on our servers.

  Web History: We access the URL of the current tab to identify supported shopping sites (e.g., Amazon, AliExpress, KSP) and trigger the advisor features.

  Personal Data: We do not collect names, addresses, or government IDs.

  Financial Data: Payments are handled securely through Lemon Squeezy. We do not store credit card numbers on our servers.
**
**3. Data Sharing****
  We do not sell, trade, or rent user data to third parties. Data is used solely to provide the AI shopping service.

**4. Security**
  We implement industry-standard security measures to protect the limited data we process.

**5. Contact**
  For any questions regarding privacy, please contact us via our GitHub repository
